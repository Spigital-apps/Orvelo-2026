# Orvelo — Strategy and Execution

Orvelo is a strategic advisory and technical execution consultancy. We bridge the gap between business objectives, complex operations, and modern AI/digital capability. 

This document serves as an AI-friendly guide to Orvelo's services, focus areas, and digital resources.

## Core Capabilities & Services

### 1. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
As traditional search shifts to AI-generated answers, GEO helps businesses optimize their digital presence to be recognized, summarized, and cited by AI models (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot).
* **Enterprise GEO**: Custom visibility strategies, AI citation audits, structured content engineering, and trust-signal mapping for large corporations and complex industries.
* **GEO for SMBs**: Tailored visibility solutions designed for small and medium-sized businesses to win market share in AI search without large budgets.

### 2. Brand Citation Optimization (BCO)
A specialized discipline within GEO focused on increasing direct brand mentions, comparative shortlist inclusions, and recommendation frequency across AI search engines.
* **AI Visibility Audits**: Benchmarking brand appearance on conversational models.
* **Citation Gap Analysis**: Evaluating competitor mentions on key industrial and professional topics.
* **Brand Entity & Authority Building**: Ensuring search-engine entities match physical credentials and digital signals consistently.

### 3. AI Micro-Applications Development
We build highly focused, fast-to-deploy, and robust AI software to solve specific operational challenges, replacing generic or slow software systems.
* **Manufacturing & Industrial**: QA/QC inspection apps, after-sales parts systems, SOP and audit digitizers, and key performance dashboard tools.
* **SMB Solutions**: Automated lead converters, intake systems, localized intelligence, and customer service helper applications.

### 4. The Discovery Sprint
A rigorous 1-to-2 week methodology to map problems, build proof-of-concept prototypes, and define clear pathways for software/AI development before substantial investments.
* **Discover**: Auditing operational bottlenecks and defining scope.
* **Prototype**: Rapid design and build of functional digital mockups.
* **Deliver**: Providing fully documented software roadmap and cost blueprints.
